Robert Downey Jr.

Robert John Downey Jr (RDJ), born on April 4, 1965, is an American Actor. Films that included him as the lead role have made over $14 billion worlwide. This makes RDJ one of the highest-grossing actors of all time. In 2008, RDJ finally appeared in "blockbuster" films namely: Iron Man and Topic Thunder. It was John Favreau's idea to cast RDJ as titular character saying he found most of Stark's life is similar to that of RDJ.

Aside from the Marvel Frnachise, RDJ would also appear in other films such as Sherlock Holmes (2009) and Oppenheimer (2023). Working with other well-known actors like Cillian Murphy, Jude Law and Jack Black. All of the success RDJ has done has led to him being nominated numerous times for Academy Awards, Oscars and Golden Glode Awards. Ultimately winning three Golden Globe Awards so far in his lifetime.


Iron Man and Marvel

Upon the release of Iron Man in April of 2008, many reviews have cited that Downey's performance was the highlight of the film. By October 2008, RDJ agreed to star in both sequels of Iron Man, and later reprising his role in The Incredible Hulk (2008) and The Avengers (2011). RDJ would soon be known worlwide for his character as Iron Man and the Marvel Franchise. In one instance, the late Stan Lee (creator of Marvel) even said RDJ was born to play Iron Man. RDJ's last performance as Iron Man would soon be seen in the 4th Avenger's movie: Avengers: Endgame, marking an end to Iron Man's story.
