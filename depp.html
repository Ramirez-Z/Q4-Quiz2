Johnny Depp

John Christoher Depp (Johnny Depp), born on June 9, 1963, is an American actor and musician. He began his music career performming in several rock bands before stepping into the world of acting/cinema. Depp made his feature film debut in "A Nightmare on Elm Street" and "Platoon (1986)". He also acted in independent films like "Edward Scissorshand" and "Sleepy Hollow".

Depp became recognized worlwide for his role as Captain Jack Sparrow in the swashbuckler fil series "Pirates of the Caribbean"
(2003-2017). Other notable characters he portrayed are Gellert Grindelwald in "Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them"and
"Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald" and Edward Scissorhands in "Edward Scissorhands"


Pirates of the Caribbean and Awards

In 2003, Depp portrayed Jack Sparrow in Pirates of the Caribbean: Curse of the Black Pearl. The first movie in the eventual Pirates of the Caribbean movie franchise. The film also included Orlando Bloom and Keira Knightley. Depp's performace as Jack earned him his first Academy Award Nomination. Being later nominated for his role as Peter Pan in Barrie's Finding Neverland (2004). Depp will eventually reprise his role as Jack Sparrow in the othe installment in the Pirates of the Caribbean series: Dead Man's Chest(2006), At World's End(2007), On Stranger Tides(2011) and Dead Men Tell No Tales(2017). These films were among the highest-grossing films in their own times.
